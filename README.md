# saudi_cpi_analysis
# Saudi Cost of Living Analysis 🇸🇦

Analysis of Consumer Price Index (CPI) trends across Riyadh, Jeddah, 
and Dammam from 2018 to 2023.

## Key Findings
- Food was the fastest-inflating category at +30.2%
- Jeddah housing rose +23.1% vs Riyadh's +15.3%
- Education stayed near-flat (+2.2%) due to government subsidies
- Transport dipped in 2020 then surged +14.9% by 2022
